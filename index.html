FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free trial?▼
Yes! Starter is free forever, and Professional has a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.
What HVAC standards do you support?▼
ASHRAE 2021 (RTS method), NBR 16401, SMACNA, and DW144. We include 51 Brazilian weather stations.
How does the AI analysis work?▼
Qotr uses Claude AI to analyze budgets, suggest cost optimizations, and generate proposal text with HVAC engineering context.
Can I import existing projects?▼
Yes. CMXPro supports importing from our legacy desktop app, and Qotr imports from Excel spreadsheets.
What about data security?▼
All data encrypted at rest and in transit via Firebase. Role-based access control. We never share your data.
Do you offer custom AI agents?▼
Yes, through Enterprise. Custom agents for email monitoring, content automation, market tracking — running 24/7.

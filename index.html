FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a free trial?▼
Yes! The Starter plan is completely free forever, and the Professional plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
What HVAC standards do you support?▼
CMXPro Suite implements ASHRAE 2021 (RTS method), NBR 16401, SMACNA, and DW144. We include 51 Brazilian weather stations with ASHRAE climate data.
Can I import existing projects?▼
Yes. CMXPro supports importing from our legacy desktop application (250K+ lines migrated), and Qotr can import budget data from Excel spreadsheets.
How does the AI analysis work?▼
Qotr uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to analyze budgets, suggest cost optimizations, and generate proposal text. The AI has context about HVAC engineering and Brazilian market pricing.
What about data security?▼
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using Firebase infrastructure. We use role-based access control and never share your project data with third parties.
Do you offer custom AI agents?▼
Yes, through our Enterprise plan. We build custom AI agents for email monitoring, content automation, market tracking, and more — running 24/7 on dedicated infrastructure.
